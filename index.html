"The Seattle Freeze"

Newcomers feel frozen out of meeting new people

Sign up

Let's melt the "frozen feeling" and build community through new connections between Seattleites and the 5,000 newcomers who move each month to Seattle.

With a red-hot economy, Seattle is seeing a flood of new arrivals. More than 60,000 city residents — nearly one in 10 of us — moved here from outside King County within the past year. Seattle is the fastest-growing big city in the country right now, and all our newly arrived neighbors are reshaping Seattle.

Let’s Walk!

Invite a newcomer to your favorite walk -- around Green Lake or on the Burke Gilman Trail.

“Grab a Coffee”

From Starbucks to independent artisan coffee shops, Seattle is famous for it’s coffee culture. Sit down for a conversation and get to know someone new!

Community Activities

From local community centers to the City of Seattle’s P-Patch Community Gardening program, opportunities to share an activity are plentiful!
