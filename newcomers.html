Welcome

Seattle has a reputation for being polite, but not friendly to new comers. But there are long time residents who do want to connect with new comers, but don't know how. Seattle Melt is here to connect new comers like you to welcoming Seattleites.

Who Are You?

You may have just arrived to Seattle or maybe you have been here for more than a year, but still feel disconnected. Maybe you came from Portland or as far away as Australia. Seattle Melt will help you build community.

Who Are Seattleites?

They are born and raised Seattleites or long time residents who are eager to share their favorite things about Seattle with you. Connect to folks who live near you, may share similar interests, work in same industries, or share other commonalities.
